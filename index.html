The Setting

Warhammer 40,000 is a tabletop miniature wargame set in a dystopian and war-torn future. The Milky Way Galaxy is a battleground where humanity, represented by the Imperium of Man, fights for survival against a multitude of alien threats and the insidious forces of Chaos. The setting is characterized by its grim and gritty atmosphere, vast scale, and complex lore. This intricate world offers countless opportunities for storytelling and exploration.
